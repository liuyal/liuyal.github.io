About Me

I am a fourth year Computer Engineering student studying at Simon Fraser University. I have background knowledge on both software and hardware engineering development from previous course work as well as from a professional work environment.

Previously I worked as a co-op student under the Laboratory for Alternative Energy Conversion (LAEC). As a laboratory engineer, I developed multiple testbeds designed to test sustainable energy systems which utilize next-gen adsorption materials, vacuum systems, low pressure evaporators, and prototype heat exchangers to produce cooling power for high performance applications. I also developed custom data visualization and analysis software to streamline experimental data analysis. I have briefly worked on remote database design and database management for LAEC.
